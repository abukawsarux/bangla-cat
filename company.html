About Us

JOURNEY OF OVER 20 YEARS!

Being one of the successful business ventures of Caterpillar Inc. USA dealer network, we, Bangla Trac
Limited, strive to continue to provide solutions in power and infrastructure development sector for ensuring a sustainable future for Bangladesh.

WHAT MADE THE DIFFERENCE
  • CAT generators have a combined capacity of over 4,000MW, made BanglaCAT one of the largest contributors in the sector of power generation in Bangladesh.
  • Globally known as the Largest Electric Power Generation (EPG) Dealer of Caterpillar in Asia Pacific Region and is among the Top 5 Caterpillar EPG Dealers in the world.
  • Established strong foothold in the marine industry to deliver ‘One Stop High Value Solution’- majority of approved vessels of private and government organizations (BIWTA & BWDB) are operating with CAT Marine engines and parts.
  • Own the largest rental yard in the country with machine demonstration ground.
  • Our Workshop at Ashulia is recognized as "Caterpillar Certified Contamination Control facility" by Caterpillar Inc.
  • The CAT forklift trucks make up more than a quarter of the total market share of the country.
  • 24/7 Product and Service Support nationwide- Anytime, Anywhere!
  • Over 2,200 customers, 7,000 plus machines are up and running.
  • Achieved ‘Strong Silver’ accolade in Caterpillar’s worldwide Service Excellence Program (SEP), earned us the highest standard in entire Asia Pacific region.
  • Achieved “0” Recordable Injury Frequency (RIF) presenting our commitment to ensure best safety culture across the company.
  • Largest Excavator, Dozer and Wheel Loader supplier in Goverment Project & variouse Industrial & Construction Site.
